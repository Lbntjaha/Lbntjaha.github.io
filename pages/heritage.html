A STORY IN EVERY GEMSTONE

Our Approach.
Our unique approach combines a long family history in the gem trade with an innovative approach to 21st-century business. Our business model combines our passion and love for every gemstone with a highly selective and rigorous approach to gem identification and classification.

We get to know our exclusive clientele to assist in making our clients investments in gemstones unique and individual to every buyer. We consult globally to deliver solutions that have a proven track record of fitting the right gemstone to the right buyer.
We are a leading source of knowledge, standards, and education in gemstones and are at the forefront of sharing this knowledge internationally to develop this historical and beautiful trade.


We are sustainable and care for the communities that we work in, investing in their future and the future of our beloved gemstone trade.
