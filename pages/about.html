About

Our story

At Gemhub we specialize in the mid to high-level stone market
Offering precious to semi-precious gemstones to our clients. Focusing on sourcing the highest quality gemstones, we deliver perfection in the fine art of cutting and polishing. Our business is international and consists of a number of professional gem dealers and gemologists. We work in all sectors of gem trade from responsibly mining/sourcing raw gems to cutting and polishing to produce final products, as well as certification.

Offering precious to semi-precious gemstones to our clients. Focusing on sourcing the highest quality gemstones, we deliver perfection in the fine art of cutting and polishing. Our business is international and consists of a number of professional gem dealers and gemologists.
We work in all sectors of gem trade from responsibly mining/sourcing raw gems to cutting and polishing to produce final products, as well as certification. We are a leader in gemstone knowledge, sharing and consulting for a wide array of global clientele, target investors, traders, jewelers and international companies. We offer a gemstone advisory service, including comprehensive investment advice based on your needs and tailored investment strategies and solutions for gemstones and jewelry.


We offer something for everyone; our customers range from the everyday gift buyer to exclusive high-end buyers in the market for something extraordinary and unique.

GEMSLEGACYS' VISION

our vision

Our vision at Gemhub is to be at the forefront of the gemstone trade; to work in partnership with people and the environment to bring one of the world’s most elegant beauties to people who adore them. Positioned as world leaders in the gemstone trade we will provide transparency in our work and employ only responsible and trustworthy mining practices that benefit the environment, while being progressive in nature to the evolving world in which we live.

GEMSLEGACYS' MISSION

OUR MISSION

Gemhub’s mission is to propel the gemstone trade into a new era; one that continues the extensive exploration of the finest gems and brings them to our beloved clientele.
